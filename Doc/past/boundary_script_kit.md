# Boundary Script Kit

Copy-paste scripts for protecting your time, money, energy, and relationships without sounding harsh.

## How To Use These Scripts

Pick the closest script. Replace anything inside brackets. Keep the message short. Do not add extra reasons unless the other person truly needs logistical information.

The basic formula:

> Warmth + boundary + optional next step.

Example:

> I care about you, but I am not able to lend money right now. I hope you understand.

## Money Boundaries

### When Someone Asks To Borrow Money

```text
I care about you, but I am not able to lend money right now. I hope you understand.
```

```text
I am not in a position to lend money, but I can help you think through other options if that would be useful.
```

```text
I cannot lend money, and I do not want to make a promise I cannot keep.
```

### When Family Asks For Money

```text
I love you, and I understand why you are asking. I am not able to give money right now.
```

```text
I cannot contribute financially this time. I can help with [specific non-money support] if that helps.
```

```text
I need to stay firm with my budget, so I am not able to send money.
```

### When Someone Keeps Pushing After You Said No

```text
I understand this is stressful, but my answer is still no.
```

```text
I do not want to keep discussing it because I do not want to give you false hope. I am not able to help financially.
```

```text
I care about you, and I am still not able to lend money.
```

### When A Group Plan Is Too Expensive

```text
That sounds fun, but it is outside my budget right now. I am going to skip this one.
```

```text
I am keeping my spending lower this month, so I cannot join for [event/trip/dinner]. I would love to do something simpler another time.
```

```text
I am not comfortable spending that much right now. Please go ahead without me, and I hope you have a great time.
```

### When You Are Asked To Split Costs Unfairly

```text
I am happy to pay for what I ordered, but I am not comfortable splitting the full bill evenly this time.
```

```text
I planned for my portion, so I am going to cover my own order separately.
```

```text
I want to be clear before we book: I can contribute [amount], but I cannot go above that.
```

### When A Partner Expects You To Cover More

```text
I need us to talk about money more clearly. I am not comfortable continuing to cover this much without a shared plan.
```

```text
I can cover [specific amount/expense], but I need you to own [specific amount/expense] going forward.
```

```text
I want us to feel like a team, and that means the financial responsibility needs to be clearer and more balanced.
```

## Social Boundaries

### Declining An Invitation

```text
Thank you for inviting me. I cannot make it this time, but I hope you have a lovely time.
```

```text
I would love to see you, but I do not have the capacity this week. Can we plan something quieter another time?
```

```text
I am going to sit this one out and recharge. Thank you for thinking of me.
```

### Declining Without Explaining

```text
I cannot make it, but thank you for inviting me.
```

```text
That will not work for me, but I hope it goes well.
```

```text
I am not available then. Thank you for understanding.
```

### Cancelling Plans Kindly

```text
I am sorry for the change, but I need to cancel tonight. I do not have the energy to show up properly, and I would rather be honest than force it.
```

```text
I need to reschedule. I know that is inconvenient, and I appreciate your understanding.
```

```text
I cannot make it today after all. Can we look at [two options] instead?
```

## Work Boundaries

### Boss Adds More Work

```text
I can take this on, but I will need to reprioritize. Should I pause [Task A] or move [Task B] to next week?
```

```text
I want to make sure I deliver this well. With my current workload, I can either complete [existing priority] or shift focus to [new request]. Which should come first?
```

```text
I do not have room to add this without moving something else. Can you confirm what should be deprioritized?
```

### Coworker Tries To Hand Off Their Work

```text
I cannot take this on, but I can point you to the document/process that may help.
```

```text
I am focused on my own deadlines today, so I am not available to cover this.
```

```text
I can answer one specific question, but I cannot own the task.
```

### Meeting Or Message Outside Work Hours

```text
I do not check work messages after [time], but I will look at this tomorrow morning.
```

```text
I am offline for the evening. I will respond during work hours tomorrow.
```

```text
I am not available for a meeting outside my work hours. Please send a few times during [work window].
```

### Saying No Without Sounding Unhelpful

```text
I cannot own this, but I can help clarify the next step.
```

```text
I am not the right owner for this. I recommend routing it to [person/team].
```

```text
I do not have capacity to take this on, but I can review it once [specific part] is ready.
```

## Household And Partner Delegation

### Delegating Full Ownership

```text
I am carrying too much of the planning right now. Can you fully own [task] this week, including noticing what needs to happen, planning it, doing it, and following up?
```

```text
I need you to take full ownership of [task], not just wait for me to remind you. Can you handle the planning and execution from start to finish?
```

```text
I do not want to be the manager of this task. I need you to own it completely.
```

### When They Say "Just Tell Me What To Do"

```text
The part that is exhausting is having to notice, plan, and delegate everything. I need you to take ownership without me assigning every step.
```

```text
I appreciate that you want to help, but I need partnership, not task management from me.
```

```text
If I have to list every step, the mental load is still mine. I need you to handle the whole task.
```

### Resetting An Unequal Pattern

```text
I want us to reset how we divide things. I am carrying too much of the invisible planning, and it is not sustainable for me.
```

```text
I need us to divide responsibilities by full ownership, not by me planning and you helping.
```

```text
I am feeling overwhelmed because I am tracking too many things alone. Can we choose clear owners for the recurring tasks?
```

## Family Boundaries

### Guilt-Tripping Family Member

```text
I love you, and I am still not available for that.
```

```text
I understand you are disappointed. My answer is still no.
```

```text
I care about you, but I cannot make this decision based on guilt.
```

### Last-Minute Family Request

```text
I cannot rearrange my day on short notice. Please ask me earlier next time.
```

```text
I am not available today. I need more notice for requests like this.
```

```text
I cannot help with this today, but I can help on [specific day/time].
```

## Emotional Labor Boundaries

### Friend Wants A Heavy Conversation

```text
I care about you, but I do not have the emotional capacity for a heavy conversation tonight. Can we talk on [day/time]?
```

```text
I want to give you real attention, and I cannot do that well right now. Can we check in tomorrow?
```

```text
I am not in the right headspace to support you properly tonight. I love you, and I will follow up on [day].
```

### You Are Becoming The On-Call Therapist

```text
I care about you, but I cannot be available for crisis-level conversations every day.
```

```text
I want to support you, and I also need limits so I do not burn out.
```

```text
This feels bigger than what I can hold as a friend. I think it would be good to bring in more support.
```

## Pushback Scripts

### If They Say "But I Really Need You"

```text
I hear that. I am still not available.
```

```text
I understand this matters to you. I cannot be the person for this right now.
```

### If They Say "You Always Say No Now"

```text
I am learning to be more honest about my limits. That does not mean I care less.
```

```text
I know this may feel different, but I am trying to stop overcommitting.
```

### If They Say "Fine, Never Mind"

```text
I can tell you are upset. My answer is still the same, and I am open to talking when we are both calmer.
```

```text
I do not want this to become a fight. I am going to stay with my boundary.
```

### If They Keep Negotiating

```text
I do not want to keep going back and forth. My answer is no.
```

```text
I have already thought about it, and my answer is still no.
```
